Seavion

Seavion is an independent air-sea awareness platform built for pilots, seafarers, and drone operators across the Maldives. It visualizes water runways, anchorage zones, and restricted airspace on one intuitive map to enhance operational safety and awareness.

Our beta version uses Google Earth and Mapbox integration. Subscription access with live updates and role-specific layers is coming soon.
